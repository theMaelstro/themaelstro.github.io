Keyflag Calculator

How to use

In corresponding fields enter the number of key quests you have completed and check urgent box.

Examples

Lets say you done 3 and there are total of 6 on HR1, you enter 3, you dont check urgent box.

If you completed 6 and there are 6 on HR1, you enter 6, if you completed urgent check a box for HR1.


You do it accordingly for any further ranks.

Lets take another example: Seph Rebalance 2.1.

We want HR3 quest Guardian of the Abyss (Taikun Zamuza)..

We enter data in a way shown in the picture and press Calculate.

Our result it feff1d0000000000, we have just completed Guardian of the Abyss (Taikun Zamuza).
